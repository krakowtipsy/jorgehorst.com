Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Jorge Horst ("I", "me") handles information when you visit jorgehorst.com (the "Website"). I respect your privacy and am committed to protecting any data you share with me. This policy follows GDPR principles even with minimal data collection.

What Data I Collect

This Website collects minimal data. Specifically:

  • Contact form submissions: If you fill in the contact form, I receive your name, email address, selected topic, and message. This data is used solely to respond to your inquiry.
  • Analytics: This site may use privacy-first, cookieless analytics to understand visitor patterns (such as page views and referral sources) without collecting personal information or using tracking cookies.

I do not collect, store, or process any other personal data through this Website.

How I Use Your Data

Any data you submit through the contact form is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or request
  • Discuss potential collaboration or services

I will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

This Website does not use tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party cookies. If privacy-first analytics are implemented, they operate without cookies and do not collect personally identifiable information.

Third-Party Services

This Website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: Fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which may log your IP address according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Form processing: If a third-party form service is used (such as Web3Forms or Formspree), your contact form submission is processed through their servers. These services have their own privacy policies.

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry and for reasonable follow-up. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting me.

Your Rights

Under GDPR and applicable privacy laws, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data I hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact me at hi@jorgehorst.com.

Security

This Website is served over HTTPS to ensure encrypted communication between your browser and the server. I take reasonable measures to protect any data you share with me, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

External Links

This Website may contain links to external sites (LinkedIn, X/Twitter, Facebook, etc.). I am not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. I encourage you to review their privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
