Who Is Jorge Horst?

Jorge Horst is an SEO & AI-Driven Link Building Specialist based in Poland. Jorge Horst specializes in elevating organic rankings through data-backed strategies, helping businesses increase online visibility through strategic link-building, SEO optimization, and AI-powered analysis.

Jorge Horst is an SEO Specialist at ALK, where he conducts in-depth keyword research for multilingual SEO campaigns, delivers data-driven recommendations for optimizing website content, meta tags, and structured data, and develops link-building strategies including outreach, digital PR, and creation of high-value assets.
